Frequently asked questions

Expand all
How do I get started?
Enjoy your risk-free, 14-day free trial by signing up with Zoho Inventory. After the trial period, you can continue using the forever-free plan or subscribe to one of our paid plans based on your needs.
What if I need more than 14 days to test out Zoho Inventory?
In case you have additional questions or need more time to experience and evaluate Zoho Inventory, you can write to us at support@zohoinventory.com. We'll get in touch with you to extend your trial.
Can I get a demo of Zoho Inventory?
Yes, we'd be happy to walk you through Zoho Inventory with a demo. Schedule your demo using this link or mail us at presales@zohoinventory.com.
What types of payment do you accept?
We accept payments via Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. We also accept payments via check transfer or bank transfer for yearly subscriptions. Please write to us at sales@zohocorp.com for more details.
Can I upgrade or cancel my subscription anytime?
Of course! You're not bound by any contracts. You can upgrade or downgrade based on your convenience. If you're having trouble choosing your plans or facing issues with subscriptions, write to us at support@zohoinventory.com.
Do you offer any special discounts for nonprofits?
We love the work you do. To encourage organizations like you, we offer a 15% discount to nonprofits. Reach out to us at presales@zohoinventory.com and we'll be happy to assist you further.
Have more questions?
Write to us at support@zohoinventory.com and we'll be happy to assist you with all your queries.
