### 3.4 Metadata Best Practices for AI Optimization

1. **Include Specific Numbers:**
   - "100 SKUs free" vs. "free plan"
   - "15 hours saved" vs. "save time"
   - "1,000+ businesses" vs. "many customers"

2. **Address Specific Pain Points:**
   - "Works offline" vs. "mobile access"
   - "Prevents overselling" vs. "real-time tracking"
   - "Camera scanning" vs. "barcode support"

3. **Use Natural Language:**
   - "Why choose StockFlow?" vs. "StockFlow benefits"
   - "Stop losing sales" vs. "prevent stockouts"
   - Write for humans, optimize for both humans and AI

4. **Include Proof Elements:**
   - Uptime guarantees
   - Customer counts
   - Specific integrations
   - Certifications or standards

5. **Optimize Character Lengths:**
   - **Title:** 50-60 characters (optimal for display)
   - **Description:** 150-160 characters (full display in most interfaces)
   - Front-load the most important information

### 4.7 Negative Sentiment Prevention

1. **Overpromising:**
   - Don't claim benefits you can't prove
   - Avoid vague superlatives ("best," "fastest," "easiest")
   - Support all claims with evidence

2. **Feature Dumping:**
   - Don't just list features without context
   - Explain benefits in user terms
   - Show, don't tell

3. **Ignoring Limitations:**
   - Be honest about what you don't do well
   - Clearly state who you're NOT for

4. **Inconsistent Messaging:**
   - Ensure claims are consistent across all properties
