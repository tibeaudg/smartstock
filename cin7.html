Pricing FAQs

What are the pricing tiers for Cin7 Core?

Cin7 Core offers three tiers: Standard, Pro, and Advanced. Each plan is designed to meet the needs of growing businesses, with increasing features and scalability. Pricing starts at $349/month — see above for details.

How is Cin7 Omni priced?

Cin7 Omni is a custom solution designed for larger enterprises with advanced needs. Pricing is tailored to your specific requirements, ensuring you only pay for what you use. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Does Cin7 integrate with my current systems?

Cin7 integrates seamlessly with over 700 platforms, including QuickBooks, Shopify, Amazon, and more. These integrations are included in most plans, ensuring your systems work together effortlessly.

What are the core inventory modules?

The core modules are Products, Purchases, Insights, Stock, Accounting, Production and CRM.

What are standard connections?

Standard connections include Accounting, Marketplace, Ecommerce, and Shipping connections.

What support is included with my plan?

All Cin7 plans include access to our support team and online Help Centers with 24/7 chat, knowledge base articles, and Cin7 Academy self-guided learning resources. 

Can I get more users and connections added to my plan?

Yes. Our sales team will design a solution that is just right for your business. They can explain your options and prices for extending your plan to have more users, connections, and modules.

Can I switch plans as my business grows?

Yes, Cin7’s pricing is designed to scale with you. As your business expands, you can easily upgrade to a higher tier for more robust features.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, Cin7 prides itself on transparent inventory software pricing, ensuring no unexpected costs. The costs outlined for each plan include the features listed, with no unexpected charges. Optional add-ons like advanced forecasting are available for an additional cost.

How does a customer success manager help my business?

Our Customer Success Team keeps an eye on how Cin7 is helping your business. They’re there to share resources and ideas to help you be successful and ensure you’re able to take full advantage of all Cin7 offers. While day-to-day questions and issues are best handled by our customer support team, your CSM is available to better understand your strategy and point you in the right direction.

Do I need developer APIs?

Our most advanced and fast-growing customers have internal or contracted developers that are building bespoke integrations to Cin7. There are nearly endless possibilities to develop integrations between Cin7 and other software and trading partners. Our developer APIs enable you to unlock this potential.
